This is a [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/) project bootstrapped with [`create-next-app`](https://github.com/vercel/next.js/tree/canary/packages/create-next-app).

## Getting Started

First, Install the dependencies

```bash
npm install
```

Second, run the development server:

```bash
npm run dev
```

Open [http://localhost:3000](http://localhost:3000) with your browser to see the result.

## About this Application

Welcome to our Twitter-inspired platform, where you can immerse yourself in a familiar social media experience reminiscent of Twitter's earlier days. Here's what you can do:

### Tweet and Engage:

- **Create Tweets:**
  - Share your thoughts, updates, and more through personalized tweets.
  - Engage with a dynamic community by liking, retweeting, and leaving comments on tweets.

- **Retweets and Quotes:**
  - Amplify interesting content by retweeting tweets to your followers.
  - Add your own perspective with quote tweets, fostering meaningful conversations.

### Profile Customization:

- **Edit Your Profile:**
  - Personalize your presence with ease in our dedicated profile editing section.
  - Adjust your name, choose a profile picture, and set a banner to reflect your preferences.

### Social Connectivity:

- **Follow Your Contacts:**
  - Connect with friends, colleagues, and others by following them.
  - Keep track of your followers and the likes your tweets receive.

### Insights and Analytics:

- **Statistical Module:**
  - Gain valuable insights into your social media presence.
  - View the number of likes and analyze their trends on a daily and weekly basis.

Experience the essence of past Twitter aesthetics while enjoying the enhanced features of our platform. Join us and start sharing your thoughts, retweeting, and engaging in vibrant conversations within our community!
